The Journey

4 Days / 3 Nights Expedition

01

Arrival & Transit

Arrival in Sumbawa Besar

Pick up from Sultan Muhammad Kaharuddin Airport. Transfer to your premium hotel in Sumbawa Besar. Evening briefing over a traditional local dinner at the rooftop bar.

02

The Encounter

Saleh Bay Sunrise

02:30 AM departure to Labuan Jambu. Boarding our traditional boat to reach the Bagan platforms by dawn.

  • • 3-4 hours of snorkeling with Whale Sharks
  • • Breakfast served on the Bagan platform
  • • Afternoon visit to the mangrove conservation area
03

Island Exploration

Moyo Island Hidden Waterfalls

Cruise to the legendary Moyo Island. Trek through the tropical forest to reach Mata Jitu Waterfall, once visited by Lady Diana. Snorkeling at Takat Segele on the return leg.

04

Departure

Farewell & Transfer

Morning at leisure for souvenir shopping in Sumbawa Besar. Final airport transfer for your flight back to Bali or Lombok.
