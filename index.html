The Journey

4 Days / 3 Nights Full Itinerary

01

Arrival & Immersion

Sumbawa Gateway

Airport pickup and transfer to our seaside boutique hotel. Evening briefing at the waterfront followed by an authentic Sumbawan welcome feast under the stars.

02

The Main Event

Giants of Saleh Bay

A 4:00 AM departure to reach the bagan platforms as the sun rises. Experience respectful, intimate swims with whale sharks. Return for a gourmet lunch and restorative spa afternoon.

03

Island Hopping

Moyo & Mata Jitu

Cruising to Moyo Island. A jungle trek leads to the famous Mata Jitu waterfalls, followed by snorkeling in the pristine coral gardens of Takat Sagele.

04

Farewell

Legacy of the Bay

A slow breakfast at the resort and a final visit to a local weaving village for artisanal souvenirs before transfer to the airport for your flight home.
